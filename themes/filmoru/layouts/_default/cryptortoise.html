クリプトータス

Cryptortoise

Plug in your FIDO2 key to unlock everything.
Pull it out to lock everything.

Hardware-key-based directory encryption for Linux. Zero passwords. Ever again.

⬡

No Passwords

Your FIDO2 hardware key derives encryption keys on demand. Nothing to remember. Nothing to leak.

⟐

Physical Security

Key not plugged in? Everything is encrypted. Walk away from your desk with your key and your data locks itself.

◈

One Key. Done.

A single hardware key replaces all your passwords. Pre-flashed and ready to plug in.

仕組み

How It Works

KEY PLUGGED IN
USB detected
▼
FIDO2 HMAC key derived
▼
gocryptfs mounts vaults
▼
Symlinks restored
▼
UNLOCKED
KEY REMOVED
USB removal detected
▼
Keys wiped from memory
▼
gocryptfs unmounts
▼
Symlinks removed
▼
LOCKED

Features
